Gathering Details

The Knutson's and Dunn's are opening the property up for a relaxed evening on the farm with friends, family, and neighbors. Bring your own food, drinks, chairs, blankets, and whatever makes the night comfortable.

We did this several years ago and had a wonderful time. With our country’s 250th birthday coming up, it felt fitting to give it another go.
